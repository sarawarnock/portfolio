About

Hi, I’m Sara, a fullstack web developer and graduate of the Fullstack Web Development Program at Thinkful. I am especially interested in applying all of the skills I’ve learned at Thinkful in a job in the environmental sector. I am looking to put my Environmental Studies degree to use, and I believe with the addition of problem solving and software engineering skills, I’ll be able to do just that.

I have always been a people person; I look forward to working on a team and collaborating with other developers! When I’m not learning to code, you can find me at the gym (both coaching and working out) or at a live music performance.

As a life-long learner, I am constantly looking to challenge myself to acquire new skills. I am currently learning how to play the piano and read music!
